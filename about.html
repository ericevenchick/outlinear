What is Outlinear?

Outlinear is an outliner. It's good for taking notes, keeping track of tasks and subtasks, or storing anything in bullet point form.

Outlinear syncs to Dropbox so that your outlines are available from anywhere. If you're not connected to Dropbox, it will save content locally instead.

Key Bindings

  • Up - Move Up
  • Down - Move Down
  • Enter - Insert a New Line
  • Tab - Increase line indent
  • Shift+Tab - Decrease line indent
  • Control+Up - Move line up
  • Control+Down - Move line down
  • Delete - Delete line

Dropbox Sync

Click 'login with Dropbox' and authorize the app to start syncing with Dropbox. When 'connected' is displayed in the top right corner, you're working on outlines in your Dropbox.

Outlinear only has access to its App subdirectory in your Dropbox. It can't access any other data.
